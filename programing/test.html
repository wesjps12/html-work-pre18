Our Mission

To successfully partner with Indian tribes in the Pacific Northwest -- to work together to enhance the lives of our communities.

Our Vision

To build houses; to build Tribal community centers; to build health facilities; to build workspaces that encourage creativity, secular growth and pride in accomplishments. To build strong communities where proud children grow in a safe environment with a solid foundation

Our Goal ission

To be invited and awarded bids for future building projects. To prove our capabilities and beautify the native communities of our homeland.
