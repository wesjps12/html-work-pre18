Our Capabilities

Native Land Development, LLC




In 2009, after operating in the Portland, Oregon area for 20+ years, Native Land Development was created as an expansion of our existing sister company, allowing us to focus solely on Tribal organizations so as to enhance the lives of fellow Native Americans.

Full-scale construction from pre-planning to key delivery. Including: • Partnership with A&E firms to ensure authentic partnership
• TERO partnership to establish hiring opportunities for tribal members
• Site development (including all a street & traffic management that compliments existing communities)
• Earthwork, utility, hardscaping, structure construction, exterior & interior finishing... foundation to roof completion
•Extensive knowledge of standardized building code requirements, inspection expectation & federal compliance•Work experience with green building, Energy Star, ADA compliance, OSHA, WISHA, Washington State L& I, BIA, NEPA and all local applicable laws.

Construction Management Skills:

Native Land Development practices a full-scale construction management & administration philosophy. This style allows for Tribal Administrative Personnel to maintain a close relationship with the project, while continuing to achieve the daily duties of their position. Having overseen over 300+ construction projects, we have honed a process that alleviates unnecessary time loss, controls unexpected costs & delivers a quality product in a timely fashion. Effective Construction Project Management includes cost & schedule control

Contingent upon each contract, Tribal Administrative Authorities and/or Project Team Members can chose a program or ála carte processes that best fit the Tribe’s needs. Some key steps in our process include:•Construction Quality Control: Proven successful steps that ensure mutual & thorough understanding of the contractual agreement & project expectations
•Fiscal Management Executive to ensure budget compliance on an on-going basis, as well potential cost saving options
•Construction Manager to ensure progress is timely, work performed is satisfactory, productivity goals are met and future problems are proactively resolved
•Public involvement facilitation to ensure all owners & stakeholders are supportive of project plans, goals and processes. Continuing progress meetings can be arranged to communicate progress and achievements while offering an open discussion to all affected parties
•Excellent ability to effectively communicate with all engineering and architectural professionals
